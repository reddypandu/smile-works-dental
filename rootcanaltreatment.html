ROOT CANAL TREATMENT

A 'Root Canal Treatment 'is used to repair and save a tooth that is badly decayed or become infected. During root canal procedure, the infected nerve, blood vessels and pulp tissue are removed from tooth after which it is cleaned and sealed by biocompatible material. Without treatment, tissue surrounding the tooth will become infected, resulting in pus formation known 'abscess'.

At Devansh Radiology & Imaging Clinic we practice painless root canal treatment with lesser chances of relapse and failure with maximum patient comfort. We have advance facility for doing root canal treatment at our clinic which has benefited many patients and will benefit many others.
